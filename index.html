Frequently Asked Questions

Is the market data real-time?

Intrinsic fetches end-of-day data for most metrics and real-time quotes when available via standard public financial APIs. It's designed for long-term investors, not day traders.

Which stock markets are supported?

We support only US major global exchanges including NYSE, NASDAQ. Simply type the ticker.

Is my financial data private?

Yes, absolutely. All calculations and assumption inputs happen locally on your Mac. We do not track your portfolio or your watchlists.

Do I need to be a finance expert?

No. While the tools are professional-grade (DCF, PEG), the interface is designed to be intuitive. We provide tooltips and context for every input.
